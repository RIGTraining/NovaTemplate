Eligibility

Who can rent

Renters must be at least 21 years old, possess a valid driver license, and pass identity verification before booking.

Rates

Transparent pricing

Daily, hourly, and distance-based charges are shown before confirmation. Taxes and insurance fees are calculated during checkout.

Cancellation

Flexible policy

Cancel up to 24 hours before pickup for a full refund. Late cancellations may incur a fee of up to 50% of the booking value.

1

Booking and Reservation Rules

All bookings must be completed through the NOVA dashboard. The vehicle must be collected from the selected location during the scheduled pickup window. If you arrive late, please notify support immediately to avoid charges.

2

Eligibility and Required Documents

Drivers must present a valid government-issued license, proof of address, and the credit card used for booking. International renters may be required to supply additional identification.

3

Vehicle Condition and Damage

Inspect your vehicle before departure. Report any pre-existing damage immediately. You are responsible for damage or loss during your rental period, including resurfacing fees and cleaning charges.

4

Insurance and Liability

Insurance coverage is included with every booking. Liability limits are outlined in your rental agreement. Optional coverage upgrades are available during checkout.

5

Payment and Fees

Payment is captured at booking time. Additional charges may apply for mileage overages, refueling, tolls, or failure to return the vehicle on time.

6

Contact and Support

For questions or emergency assistance, contact the NOVA support team any time. Use the in-app chat or email support@nova-ev.com.
